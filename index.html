About Us

A fire safety risk assessment is an organised and methodical look at the premises, the activities within the premises to prevent the people from any harm who use them, from a fire prevention perspective. It’s about understanding the potential risks, then improving your fire safety precautions to keep people safe. Being a fire risk assessor it is important for you reduce the risk of fire to keep people safe. It saves lives, it’s your legal duty, and it makes good business sense

Key steps for Red Fire Safety :

  • Identify the fire hazards.
  • Identify people at risk.
  • Evaluate, remove or reduce the risks.
  • Record your findings, prepare an emergency plan and provide training.
  • Review and update the Red Fire Safety regularly.

COMBINED HEALTH & SAFETY/ Red Fire SafetyS

The objective of a red fire safety is to produce a flexible, dynamic system that establishes and maintains fire safety in a premises and its vicinity. Every premises generates hazards and risks, and to ensure the safety of all persons who visit the premises. We are now able to offer Red Fire Safetys to PAS 79 standards as recognised by the Institute of Fire Safety Managers (IFSM).

FIRE ALARM SYSTEMS

The purpose of a fire alarm system is to alert building occupants in the early stages of a fire condition. It is essential this equipment is correctly maintained and regularly tested in accordance with BS 5839. We are able to maintain and repair all types of commercial and domestic fire alarm systems. We also offer a 24/7 out of hours call out service.

EMERGENCY LIGHTING

Emergency lighting is a vital part of your building. This is designed to illuminate in the event of a power failure to aid safe escape from the building. Emergency lighting needs to comply to BS 5266 and requires periodic testing. We are able to offer regular testing and repairs of all types of emergency lighting.

SMOKE VENTILATION (AOV/MOV)

Smoke ventilation systems are designed to facilitate the safe escape of occupants in the event of a fire and also enable the fire fighters to be fought in its early stages. This systems are life safety systems and play very important role for saving people in danger.There is well defined and strict legislation and design guidance that defines and governs the installation, performance and maintenance of Smoke Ventilation systems that must be considered and adhered to when specifying any system.
